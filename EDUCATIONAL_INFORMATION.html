Welcome to Digital Ethics & Responsible Use

In our digital age, responsible technology use is crucial. Follow these core principles to ensure safety, respect, and integrity online.

Core Principles

These rules guide ethical and responsible digital behavior.

Respect Privacy

Always protect your own and others' personal information and avoid sharing sensitive data without consent.

Be Honest

Share accurate information and avoid spreading rumors or misinformation online.

Practice Courtesy

Communicate respectfully, avoiding harassment, bullying, or offensive language.

Secure Your Accounts

Use strong passwords, enable 2FA, and be vigilant about cybersecurity practices.
